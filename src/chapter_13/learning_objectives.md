## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:

1. Describe the four generations of software — 1.0 through 4.0 — and explain how each generation changed what software engineering required.
2. Identify the failure modes that emerge when traditional SE practices are applied without modification to AI systems.
3. Name the emerging specialist roles in AI-era software engineering and describe the responsibilities of each.
4. Explain what SE4AI is as a discipline, distinguishing what it inherits from traditional SE and what it introduces as genuinely new.
5. Describe the scope and purpose of each of the three SE4AI sub-disciplines: MLOps, LLMOps, and AgentOps.
6. Map the SE4AI lifecycle loop and explain what happens when any stage of that loop is skipped.
